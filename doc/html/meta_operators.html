Meta operators

  • opBlockDiag - Operator-diagonal operator
  • opBlockOp - Blockwise application of operator on matrices
  • opCTranspose - Conjugate transpose of an operator
  • opConj - Take the elementwise conjugate of a complex operator
  • opDiag - Diagonal operator
  • opDictionary - Dictionary of concatenated operators
  • opExcise - Excise rows or columns of an operator
  • opExtend - Symmetric extension operator
  • opFoG - Forms the product of two operators
  • opImag - Complex imaginary part of operator
  • opInverse - (Pseudo) inverse of operator
  • opKron - Kronecker tensor product
  • opMask - Selection mask
  • opMinus - Difference of two operators
  • opOrthogonal - Abstract class for orthogonal operators
  • opPInverse - Pseudo inverse of operator
  • opPower - Raise operator to integer power
  • opReal - Real part of operator
  • opRestriction - Restriction operator
  • opSpot - Spot operator super class
  • opStack - Stack of vertically concatenated operators
  • opSubsAsgn - Redefine rectangular subset of operator
  • opSubsRef - Extract rectangular subset of operator entries
  • opSum - Addition of two operators
  • opTranspose - Transpose of an operator
  • opUnaryMinus - Negation of an operator
